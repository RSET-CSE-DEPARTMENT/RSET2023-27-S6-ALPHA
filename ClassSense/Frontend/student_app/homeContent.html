Welcome to ClassSense

ClassSense is a smart classroom engagement system designed to help students stay focused during online sessions. By analyzing participation and attention patterns, the system provides meaningful insights that help students understand their engagement levels and improve their learning experience.

📊 Engagement Insights

ClassSense evaluates your activity during meetings and generates an attention score to help you understand your engagement level in each session.

🔒 Privacy Protection

Monitoring occurs only during active classes and collected data is securely stored for academic engagement analysis.

📈 Self Improvement

Session reports allow you to track attention scores, alerts, and participation duration to improve focus in future classes.

How to Use ClassSense
  • Use the Join Meeting option in the navigation bar.
  • Enter the meeting code provided by your instructor.
  • Participate normally during the class session.
  • ClassSense analyzes engagement and attention during the session.
  • After the session ends, visit the Reports section.
  • Review your attention score, duration, and alerts.
